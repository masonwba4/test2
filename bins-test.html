Have Your Say: Community Governance Review 2025

Thank you to everyone who took part in the first stage of the Community Governance Review (CGR). The initial consultation received 50 responses from residents and parish councils across 22 of the 27 parish council areas. 

We’ve now completed Stage 2, where the Council’s Standards and Resources Committee reviewed all the submissions received and decided that one proposal should go forward for further consultation in Stage 3 running from Friday 31st October until Tuesday 31st December 2025. 

If you live in the former West Ward area of Penkridge Parish Council, this is your chance to have your say on the proposed changes. Please take a few minutes to complete our online survey and share your views.

You can read all the anonymised responses from Stage 1 on our webpage here: Have your say: Community Governance Review 2025 | South Staffordshire District Council

If you have any questions, feel free to contact us at elections@sstaffs.gov.uk.
